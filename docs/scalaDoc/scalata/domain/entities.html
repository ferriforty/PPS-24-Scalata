Hostile creature.

Hostile creature.

  • enemyType – family/glyph (goblin, pig, …).

  • position – current tile.

  • health / maxHealth – hit-points pool.

  • attackPower – raw damage inflicted to a player.

All mutators return a new Enemy; no in-place changes.
